Don't tell us what you're reading-show us!

Take a picture with a book cover as your face (be creative!), and send us your photo at lcbc.champs@gmail.com by November 7th. Remember to include your name and age in your submission.

We will choose ten winners from all entrants who have submitted an entry during the submission period and display their pictures on the LCBC homepage. Winners will be chosen based upon originality and creativity, and will be notified by email by November 8th.
