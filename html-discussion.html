The Boy On The Bridge

Discussion Questions

  1. How did Stephan's social anxiety contribute to the plot?
  2. What did you like best about the book?
  3. A good ending is key to every great book. Do you feel that the ending did the book justice?
  4. Share your favorate quote from the book. Why does this perticular quote stand out?
  5. Would you read another book by Mike Carey? Why or why not?
  6. What did you think of the book's length? If it's too long, would you cut? If too short, what would you add?
  7. Which places in the book would you like to visit?
  8. "Not every bridge should be crossed" Do you agree or disagree?
  9. If you had to trade places with one character, who would it be?
  10. If you could hear the same story from another character's point of view, which character would you choose?

Happy Reading!
