Ingredients

Salad

1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes

1/4 cup basil

1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese

175 gr coucous

1 cup water

2 tbsp olive oil

1/2 tsp sea salt

1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Dressing

1 tbsp balsamic vinigar

1 garlic clove

1/4 tsp sea salt

1/4 ground black pepper

"The Grain" - Our Couscous Salad

Directions


1. In a medium sized pot, bring your water, sea salt, and olive oil to a boil.

2. Add the couscous and stir well.

3. Turn off the heat, and cover your pot.

4. Let your couscous stand until soft (about 7 minutes) and then fluff witha fork and let cool.

5. Combine your cooked couscous, diced sun dried tomatoes, chopped basil, salt, pepper and fetta cheese in a bowl.

6. Combine the dressing: whisk together the balsamic vinigar, salt, pepper, and minced garlic in a small bowl.

7. Add the dressing to your salad and stir well.

Enjoy!
