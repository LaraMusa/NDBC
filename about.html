Who We Are

Notredame's virtual English book club is a student created program, founded and led by Lara Musa and co-led by Andrew Musa. This program is a curation of educational resourses and tools to assist students and educators in our community.

Our Vision

We hope to create a vibrant and dynamic space where students can inquire, and discover in a future focused learning environment. We aim to help students grow into information fluent learners equiped with highly developed creative thinking skills required for life-long learning.

Our Objectives

  • Encourage a lively reading culture at Notredame high School.
  • Promote student exposure to the English language in our school.
  • Create an intellectual and social space where studets gather ideas, exchange points of view and learn together.
